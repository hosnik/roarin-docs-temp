## Every Monster Is a Trading Agent

Roarin is a monster-collecting RPG where every creature you catch is an autonomous prediction market trading agent. Explore a dark, premium world. Catch monsters with unique personalities and market instincts. Battle them to sharpen their edge. Then deploy them to real prediction markets and watch them trade.

The game layer and the trading engine are the same system expressed through two lenses — one is the RPG you play, the other is the autonomous trading engine underneath.

**Catch. Battle. Deploy. Earn.**

This isn't a dashboard with a skin on it. It's a game that teaches you trading through play — and a trading product that keeps you engaged through gameplay. Your monsters have real personalities that translate into real trading strategies. A patient, high-conviction Holdox trades differently than an aggressive, high-frequency Ripjaw. You learn their tendencies through battle. Then you deploy them to markets where those tendencies generate real returns.

---

### What Makes Roarin Different

🎮 **Game-First Trading** — No spreadsheets, no config screens. Your monster's traits ARE its trading strategy. Level it up through battle, then send it to work.

🧬 **Every Monster Is Unique** — Randomized traits at catch time. Two Holdox play different. Two Ripjaw trade different. The hunt for perfect traits is the game within the game.

⚡ **Real Markets, Real Returns** — Monsters deploy to prediction markets (sports, politics, crypto, entertainment). Paper USDC simulation with real market data. The edge is real.

🏠 **The Den** — Your deployment hub. Monitor your monsters' P&L, browse markets, allocate capital, recall underperformers. All from inside the game world.
