# Vision

Roarin is a monster-collecting RPG where every monster is a prediction market trading agent.

Players explore a world, catch monsters with unique market affinities and personality traits, level them through battles, and deploy them to real prediction markets. The game layer and the trading product are the same system expressed through two lenses — one is the game you play, the other is the autonomous trading engine underneath.

The tone is degen-confident. The world knows what prediction markets are. The creatures aren't mystical — they're known entities that people work with. The aesthetic is dark/premium with gold accents, matching the Roarin brand.

---

## Why This Exists

Most trading products are boring. Dashboards, config screens, sliders. You set parameters, walk away, and check back later. There's no connection to what's happening.

Most games are empty calories. Fun to play, nothing to show for it. The time you spend doesn't compound into anything real.

Roarin fuses both. Your gameplay directly improves your trading performance. The battles you fight sharpen your monster's edge. The monsters you catch expand your market coverage. The capital you earn funds your deployments. Every minute in the game world has a purpose.

---

## The Fusion

| Game Layer | Trading Layer |
| --- | --- |
| Catch a monster | Acquire a new trading agent |
| Battle and level up | Train and improve strategy parameters |
| Monster personality traits | Trading configuration (risk, patience, conviction) |
| Deploy to The Den | Activate autonomous market trading |
| Earn paper USDC | Generate returns from prediction markets |
| Equipment items | Strategy modifiers for deployment |

The player never has to think about "configuring a trading bot." They just play the game. Their monster's traits — rolled at catch time, sharpened through battle — translate directly into trading parameters. A monster with high Conviction and low Patience becomes an aggressive, high-frequency trader. A monster with high Resilience and high Focus becomes a conservative, concentrated position holder.

You learn your monster's personality by battling with it. Then you deploy it to markets where that personality generates real returns.

---

## What We're Building Toward

**Phase 1:** Catch, battle, deploy. One monster on one market. The connection between game and trading is real.

**Phase 2:** Deep battle strategy. Coaching postures. Equipment. Multiple deployments. The game gets strategic.

**Phase 3:** World expansion. New zones, new species, new market verticals. PvP arena with spectator betting.

**Phase 4:** Social layer. Monster trading between players. Seasonal events. Live mode with real USDC.

Every phase deepens both the game and the trading product simultaneously. They're not separate roadmaps — they're the same roadmap expressed twice.
