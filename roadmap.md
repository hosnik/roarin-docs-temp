# Roadmap

Four phases. Each one deepens both the game and the trading product simultaneously. They're not separate roadmaps — they're the same roadmap expressed twice.

<figure><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hosnik/roarin-docs-temp/main/.gitbook/assets/roadmap-timeline.svg" alt="Roadmap Timeline"><figcaption><em>Four phases — from Foundation to Social &amp; Live</em></figcaption></figure>

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Beta)

**Goal:** A monster can be caught, leveled through battles, and deployed to a real sim market through The Den. The game-trading connection is live.

- 5 monster species with unique traits and market affinities
- 10-15 battle moves per species (4 equipped at a time)
- Starter choice: pick your first monster from Holdox, Ripjaw, or Median
- Full trait system — randomized at catch, permanent for life
- The Den — deploy, monitor, recall, browse markets
- Monster-to-trading-agent translation (traits → trading config)
- Capital allocation per deployment
- Paper USDC as unified currency
- Shop system for potions and capture items
- Complete world narrative rewrite — degen-confident, no tutorial fluff

**Status: In Development**

---

## Phase 2: Battle Depth

**Goal:** Battles feel distinct per monster and reward strategic thinking.

- Coaching posture system (Aggro / Steady / Guard / Wildcard)
- Affinity advantage system in battle (Sports > Entertainment > Crypto > Politics > Sports)
- Move tags and weighted selection based on posture + personality
- Equipment items — modify traits specifically for deployment
- NPC trainers with themed teams and strategic posture tendencies
- The Lab building — deep monster inspection, move management
- The Reserve building — collection management beyond active party

---

## Phase 3: World Expansion

**Goal:** The world feels alive and worth exploring.

- 2-3 new zones with level-scaled encounters
- The Proving Grounds — advanced trainers, gym-leader-style progression gates
- The Hall building — leaderboards, tier progression display
- Arena reintegration as a world building (spectator betting, unified USDC economy)
- Tier-gated deployment slots
- New monster species for new market verticals
- Public spectator mode for anonymous visitors

---

## Phase 4: Social & Endgame

**Goal:** Multiplayer and long-term retention.

- Monster-vs-monster PvP arena (AI-controlled battles, spectators bet)
- Monster trading/selling between players
- New species covering politics, crypto, and entertainment markets
- Advanced equipment and rare item drops
- Seasonal content and limited-time events
- **Live mode transition — sim to real USDC**

---

## The Principle

Every phase adds depth to both layers simultaneously. A new battle feature improves how monsters trade. A new zone adds new species that unlock new markets. A social feature creates new trading opportunities. Game and trading are one system — they evolve together.
